**Reporting a problem with Ace Citizenship**

Are you having problems with Ace Citizenship? The fastest way to get help is to email [blake@941apps.com](mailto:blake@941apps.com) with a description of the issue and, optionally, a screenshot from the app.

**Contacting the developer**

Ace Citizenship is developed by Blake Crosley for 941 Apps, LLC. Send questions to [blake@941apps.com](mailto:blake@941apps.com). Let me know if you have feature requests, suggestions, feedback, or anything you want to communicate.
